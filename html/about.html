About

Established in 2022, SS Retro Records was found by
Simanye Somdaka to minimize the gap between the analogue era and digital era.

In our inventory we store all the classics of the 60s, 70s
and 80s. Don't you miss the original quality of the
greatest classics and wish to delight your ears to the
sweet tunes as they came out over 40 years ago? If so
SS Retro Records has got you covered.

From Vinyls, Cassette Tapes, VHS Tapes, Turntables,
Tape Players and VHS Systems we have got it all!
